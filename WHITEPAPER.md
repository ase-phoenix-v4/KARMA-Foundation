## IV. PROOF-OF-KARMA (PoK)

Proof-of-Karma is a novel consensus mechanism where validator voting power is weighted by their on-chain Karma score.

- Every action is classified: Creation (+3), Neutral (0), Spam (-5).
- Karma score determines mining priority.
- A validator with negative Karma loses voting power.
- Attack becomes irrational: the attacker destroys their Karma before seizing the network.

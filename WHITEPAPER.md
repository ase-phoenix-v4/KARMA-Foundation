- Rotations every 30 days — validators who act against the network are removed.
- Creates permanent supply destruction and ensures network security.

### 2. Gravity Pool (Loyalty Rewards)
- 50% of all swap fees are **distributed to holders** who haven't sold for 30+ days.
- Early sellers **lose all accumulated rewards** (they get burned).
- Creates strong incentive to hold long-term.

### 3. Genesis Stairway (Exponential Price Curve)
- Every doubling of price (x2, x4, x8...) **automatically burns additional 2%** of purchase volume.
- Price grows exponentially, not linearly.
- Investors can predict growth trajectory based on math, not speculation.

- ✅ **Sovereign L1 Blockchain** — 3 active nodes, 10s block time
- ✅ **Proof-of-Karma Consensus** — karma-weighted mining
- ✅ **On-Chain AMM** — KARMA/USDC and KARMA/POL pools
- ✅ **Bonding Curve** — price grows with every swap
- ✅ **Burn & LP** — 10% burned, 5% to liquidity
- ✅ **Anti-Whale** — 1% max hold, 0.5% max sell/hour
- ✅ **Multisig Governance** — 2 signatures required
